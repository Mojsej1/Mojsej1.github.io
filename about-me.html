Mojsej Azmanov

Basketball takes a big chunk of my life as I practice it every single day, but I also have other things I like to do during my free time.

Strengths/Expertises

Shooting

My best skill out of all is my shooting. I can shoot off of a dribble, catch and shoot, and in pick and pops. My shooting FG percentage is 45% while my 3-pt shooting percentage is 40%.

Dribbling

Dribbling is probably the skill I work most on. I dribble a ball for at least 3 hours a day. For a point guard like me dribbling is one of the most crucial skills that I must have and I have it.

Passing

Another key attribute of a point guard is their passing. I am floor facilitator so I like to pass a lot to my open teamates. I avergae 7-8 assists per game and around 35-50 passes per game.

Hobbies Outside of Basketball

Exercizing

When I am not bouncing a basketball you could catch me in the gym doing push ups and curl ups. I try to excersize at least 4 to 5 times a day to keep myself in good shape.

Reading

Music

If I am having a bad day, I always whip out my sweet mp4 touch coral and put some bangers on it. I like hip-hop and rap, but lately I have been listening to quite a bit of Jazz.
